# Competitor Research Dimensions

- Dimensions used to benchmark competitive landscape for property managers.
- Not all information will be available or accessible, industry and/or regional benchmarks can be used to reduce gaps, assumptions are acceptable when stated.

## Goals
- Competitive benchmarking existing property management firms in King and Snohomish Counties (WA State). These separate good operators from cosmetically-good operators and are the levers a new entrant can beat them on.

## Key Metrics
- doors per property manager:
- owner churn
- days on market
- employee counts
- rent collection rate
- ancillary revenue per door
- contract terms
- Google review count
- Yelp review count

## Financial Performance

- Gross revenue / revenue per unit / revenue per door
- EBITDA margin
- Revenue mix (management fees vs leasing vs ancillary vs maintenance markup)
- Ancillary revenue per unit (pet fees, late fees, lease prep, NSF, eviction processing)
- Average management fee realized (not advertised; actual)
- Owner churn rate / annual logo churn %
- Net unit growth rate (units added minus lost, trailing 12 months)
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) per owner
- Lifetime Value (LTV) per owner / per unit
- LTV:CAC ratio
- Payback period on new owner

## Operational Performance

- Days on market (vacancy duration from listing to lease signed)
- Vacancy rate across portfolio
- Time to first showing
- Showings per vacancy
- Application-to-lease conversion rate
- Renewal rate / tenant retention rate
- Average tenancy length
- Rent collection rate (on-time, by day 5, by month-end)
- Delinquency rate / aged receivables
- Eviction rate per 100 units
- Maintenance work order cycle time (open to close)
- Emergency response time
- First-time fix rate
- Maintenance cost per unit per year
- Inspection cadence and findings closure rate
- Lease compliance / legal exposure incidents per year

## Portfolio Composition

- Unit count by property type (SFR / condo / small MF / large MF / commercial / HOA)
- Owner concentration (% of revenue from top 10 owners; key-customer risk)
- Geographic concentration / density (units per ZIP)
- Average rent per unit
- Class A / B / C mix
- Owner type mix (mom-and-pop / mid-tier / institutional)
- Average doors per owner

## Service and Pricing Depth (beyond What's listed)

- Pricing transparency (published vs quote-only)
- Contract terms (cancellation, exclusivity, lockup, holdover clauses)
- Performance guarantees (lease guarantee, pet damage, eviction protection, happiness)
- Maintenance markup transparency (disclosed vs hidden)
- Trust accounting practices (separate vs commingled)
- Owner reporting cadence and granularity
- Owner portal features depth
- Inspection types and frequency included vs upcharge
- 1099 / tax reporting included

## Technology and Process Maturity

- Core PMS (already have)
- Owner portal (yes/no, mobile, features)
- Tenant mobile app
- Online application / e-signature
- Self-showing tech (Showdigs, Tenant Turner, Rently)
- Maintenance triage automation (Property Meld, Latchel, Lula, EZ Repair Hotline)
- Vendor management system
- AI/leasing chatbot
- Communication SLAs (response time committed)
- Integrations (accounting, screening, insurance, utilities)

## Compliance and Risk

- WA DOL firm license status
- Designated broker license + tenure
- Trust account audit history
- E&O insurance carrier + limits
- General liability + cyber coverage
- Fair Housing training cadence
- Seattle-specific compliance (Fair Chance, RRIO, First-in-Time, Just Cause, 180-day notice)
- HB 1217 rent stabilization compliance posture
- Litigation history / Attorney General complaints
- BBB complaint count and resolution rate
- DOJ / HUD complaints
- Eviction lawsuits filed
- Security deposit return disputes

## Reputation and Customer Experience

- Google rating + count (not on list)
- Yelp rating + count (on list)
- Facebook rating + count
- BBB complaints (count, not just letter grade)
- Owner NPS / CSAT
- Tenant NPS / CSAT
- Review velocity (recent 12 months vs all-time)
- Owner referral rate
- Repeat-owner rate (multi-property owners with the firm)

## Team and Capacity

- Total headcount
- Property managers (number, designation level)
- Doors per property manager (workload ratio; industry healthy band 150 to 350 SFR depending on tech)
- Maintenance staff in-house vs outsourced
- Leasing agents
- Bookkeepers / trust accountants
- Tenure of leadership
- NARPM / IREM / CCIM designations held by staff (partially in list)
- Bilingual staff languages

## Growth and Corporate Development

- Acquisition activity (rolled-up firms in last 5 years)
- Founding partner exit risk / succession plan
- Investor relationships (own portfolio managed)
- Brokerage tie-in (sales + management cross-sell)
- New construction lease-up capability
- Affiliate network (insurance, lending, vendor)

## Strategic Positioning

- Stated ICP (ideal customer profile)
- Differentiation claim
- Marketing channels active (SEO, SEM, partnerships, NARPM)
- Domain authority / SEO rank for "[city] property management"
- Content velocity (blog, video, podcast)
- Social media presence (already partial)

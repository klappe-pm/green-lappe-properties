# Ltv-to-cac-ratio

## Summary

Lifetime value per owner divided by cost to acquire that owner.

## Category

Financial Performance (P1 priority)

## What it Signals

Sustainability of growth. Below 3:1 means the growth engine is uneconomic. Above 5:1 means there is room to invest more in acquisition.

## How to Interpret

If under 3:1, reduce CAC (more referrals, organic) before pursuing volume. If over 7:1, the firm is under-investing in growth.

## Benchmark

3:1 minimum; 5:1 healthy; 10:1+ exceptional.

## Example

LTV $15,000, CAC $1,500. Ratio 10:1, very healthy.

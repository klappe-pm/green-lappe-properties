# Market Research Insights - Demographics

## Summary

Top-school ZIPs are not the best PM markets. The multilingual screening moat earns its highest return in underserved South King ZIPs, while top-school Eastside ZIPs are owner-heavy, yield-compressed, and PM-saturated. Run two entities, two theses.

### Prioritized Actions

1. Form a natural-person LLC this week. Any corporate member permanently kills the HB 1217 small-landlord exemption.
2. Launch PM in `98052` and `98109`. Only ZIPs with `Δrank = 0` under ±30% weight perturbation.
3. Build multilingual screening: Mandarin → Hindi → Telugu → Russian/Ukrainian. No incumbent does this.
4. Front-load acquisitions in the next 12 months. Zero ZIPs show yield compression; a 2026–27 rate cut flips the universe.
5. Bifurcate: acquisition entity on Eastside top-school yields, PM entity on South King multilingual demand.

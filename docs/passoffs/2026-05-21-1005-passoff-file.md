# Do not do

- Do not treat the domain/trademark/registrar notes or runbook as legal, trademark, licensing, tax, or insurance advice.
- Do not buy domains without explicit partner approval and account-owner action.
- Do not assume Verisign RDAP HTTP 404 means the domains will still be available in a registrar cart.
- Do not buy speculative backup domains without explicit approval.
- Do not commit registrar credentials, receipts, personal registrant data, payment details, or sensitive screenshots to this repository unless the repository access model changes.
- Do not publish public sales or property-management service language before licensing/counsel gates clear.
- Do not configure public production content before the staging/access-control plan is implemented.

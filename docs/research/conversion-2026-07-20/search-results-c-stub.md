# search-results-c-stub

RCFE / in-home assisted care stub inputs, researched 2026-07-20. All figures carry source URLs in the agent output; flags noted inline.

- Admin cert: 80-hr ICTP + state exam (100 q, 70% pass, within 60 days). Age 21+, HS/GED, background check. Training $500-1,500; ~$100 exam + ~$140 initial cert app (secondary; confirm vs CDSS fee schedule).
- License application fee by capacity: $495 (1-3 beds), $990 (4-6), $1,486 (7-15). 6-bed = $990. FLAG: annual renewal fee unverified from primary.
- Timeline: 4-18 months realistic (4-6 favorable; 12-18 with renovation/fire-clearance stalls).
- Revenue rates SoCal: CA assisted living median ~$6,250-6,631/mo; Greater LA (incl OC) ~$6,098 (2025); Riverside ~$5,324; OC est $6,000-6,500. Board-and-care San Diego $3,500-7,500, most $4,000-6,000; budget family-run $2,500-3,500. FLAG: Genworth-derived secondary, skews to large communities; no systematic 6-bed dataset.
- Staffing: Title 22 87415: <=15 residents needs 1 on-call staff on premises at night; awake night staff only when condition warrants; 87411 allows CDSS to require more. Practical 6-bed: 1-2 day caregivers + 1 on-premises night. Wages: CA caregiver avg ~$19.77/hr (Indeed 2026); AL caregivers $16-19/hr; floor = CA min wage ($16.50 in 2025).
- Capex: sprinklers required per Title 24 for bedridden >1, non-ambulatory above first story, or 3rd story+. NFPA 13D retrofit $4-10/sqft 2025-26; 2,500 sqft home ~$5,000-15,000+. Non-ambulatory clearance needs level/direct egress (ramps, widened doors). FLAG: no delayed-egress or grab-bar cost data found.
- Occupancy: NIC Q1-2026 senior housing 89.5%, AL segment 87.9%; 19 straight quarters of gains, construction lowest since 2012. FLAG: NIC excludes 6-bed homes. Small-home supply shrinking: <=15-bed RCFE count -8% while 16+ grew +18% (CHCF); LA County -45 facilities/-1,226 beds 2016-2019. No reliable current closure-rate stat.
- Insurance: mandatory AB 1523 $1M/occ, $3M agg. Premium anecdotes $2,000-4,000/yr (2014 analysis) or ~$50/resident/mo (~$3,600/yr for 6). FLAG: 2014-era; current premiums individually underwritten, likely higher.

Stub verdict shape: 6-bed private-pay board-and-care revenue potential $24-36K/mo gross at full occupancy in OC/SD, but it is a 24/7 staffed care business (2-3 FTE + operator), 4-18 mo licensing, admin certification, sprinkler-contingent capex, and shrinking small-home cohort; structurally the most labor-intensive option. Model to structure and inputs only per prompt.

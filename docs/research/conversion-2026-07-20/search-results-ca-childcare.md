## FCCH survival / exit

- CA FCCHs -30% 2008-2017 (39K to 27.5K); LA -37%. National small FCC -48% 2005-2017. First uptick 2021-2023 (+79 homes).
- No direct 1-3 yr closure stat. Proxies: MN 12-14%/yr closure churn; FL study 19.7% 1-yr, 50.6% 5-yr business failure; subsidy participation extends survival. Model attrition on MN churn, flagged proxy.

## Operating costs

- CA min wage $16.90 (2026-01-01); LA city $17.87 to $18.42 (7/1/26); SD city $17.75. Childcare worker median (OEWS May 2025): CA $19.73/hr; LA $19.67; OC $19.40; SD $19.62; Riv $19.82. Model assistant at min+$2.50-4.00.
- Insurance: legal floor $100K/$300K or bond or affidavits (HSC 1597.531). Premiums $400-2,000/yr; model $800-2,000 for 14-cap large FCCH with $1M + abuse coverage.
- CACFP FDCH rates 7/25-6/26: Tier I breakfast $1.70 / lunch $3.22 / snack $0.96; Tier II $0.61 / $1.94 / $0.26.
- Licensing fees: orientation $25; large FCCH application $140, annual $140; small $73/$73.

## Timeline / sequencing

- Realistic 3-5 months application-to-license (no statutory 90-day mandate; EMSA class scheduling dominates). Preservice: 16-hr health+safety, LiveScan all adults, TB, orientation.
- Large FCCH experience gate: 22 CCR 102369(b)(5): 1 yr as small FCCH operator OR 1 yr center admin/director/teacher; discretionary waiver 102369(b)(5)(A). BASE CASE: Y1 small (6-8), large from Y2. Do not base-case the waiver.
- Large FCCH needs pre-licensure fire clearance (clearance itself $0; optional pre-inspection at cost).

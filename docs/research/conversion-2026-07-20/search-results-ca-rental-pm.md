## Regulatory

- AB 1482 caps Aug 2025-Jul 2026 (5%+CPI, 10% max): LA/Orange 8.0%; San Diego 8.8%; Riverside 7.5%. SFR/condo exempt if owner not REIT/corp/LLC-w-corp-member + notice given (CIV 1947.12(d)(5)). Same natural-person exemption pattern as WA HB 1217.
- City costs: LA city RSO $38.75/unit + SCEP $67.94 + JCO $31.05/yr (JCO applies to SFRs); RSO cap 3% to 2026-06-30 then 90% CPI 1-4% band. Santa Ana: cap lesser of 3% / 80% CPI (2.42% Sep25-Aug26); registry $100-104/unit. San Diego city: TPO just-cause day one, no-fault relocation 2-3 months rent; business tax $50+$5/unit. Riverside city: AB 1482 only, no local ordinance found. LA County uninc: 1.93% cap; registry $90/$30 per unit.
- Deposits: AB 12 max 1 month (2 months for small natural-person landlord <=2 properties/4 units); AB 2801 photo documentation mandatory; SB 611 fee bans.
- DRE: B&P 10130/10131(b) broker license required for third-party PM; owner self-management exempt (principal); unlicensed assistant scope (10131.01(a)(3)); salesperson under broker (10137, 10159.2); trust account B&P 10145 + Regs 2830-2835 (monthly reconciliation, $200 max broker funds).

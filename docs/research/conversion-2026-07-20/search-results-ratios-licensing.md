# search-results-ratios-licensing

CA/WA capacity, ratio, and licensing scope, primary-source verified 2026-07-20. Citations in agent output.

## California

- Small FCCH (solo licensee): 4 (all may be infants) / 6 (max 3 infants) / 7-8 conditional (max 2 infants; needs one K-enrolled child + one 6+; parent notice; landlord consent if rented; HSC 1597.44). Hard cap 8. Infant = under 2 (22 CCR 102352(i)(1)).
- Large FCCH (licensee + assistant, assistant may be 14+): 12 (max 4 infants) / 13-14 conditional (max 3 infants; K-child + 6+ child; notice; landlord consent; HSC 1597.465). Hard cap 14. No assistant present = falls to small capacity. Resident children under 10 count.
- Residency mandatory (HSC 1596.78: care in provider's own home; may be rented/leased/owned).
- SB 234 (HSC 1597.45): FCCH small AND large = by-right residential use statewide; no CUP, no business-license fee, no occupancy change, no CEQA. Does NOT cover centers.
- Center ratios (Title 22): infant 1:4 (aide config up to 1:12 sleeping); toddler component 18-30mo 1:6 group 12; preschool 1:12, teacher+aide 1:15, FQ teacher+aide 1:18; nap 1:24. NO group-size caps for infant/preschool (verified absence). Director mandatory on premises (101215.1); fire clearance mandatory (101171). Center in a residence = allowed by Title 22 but no by-right protection: local CUP (typical), E-occupancy, fire conversion.
- CA lowest-friction path: LARGE FCCH 14 kids / 2 staff (7:1 effective). Caps: 14 ceiling, infant limits, residency, seat-13/14 composition conditions.

## Washington

- Family home (WAC 110-300-0355), capacity tied to licensee experience: <1 yr solo = 6 (max 3 under-2, 1 walking); 1-2 yrs solo = 8 (ages 2-12, max 4 under-3); 1-2 yrs + assistant = 9 (max 4 under-2); 2+ yrs solo = 10 (3-12) or 12 school-age-only; 2+ yrs + assistant = 12 (max 6 under-2, >=2 walking). Second-staff triggers: >6 w/ any under-2; >8 w/ any under-3; >10 w/ any under-school-age.
- Waiver 13-16 (WAC 110-300-0358, intake from Apr 2025): nonexpiring license, 3+ yrs experience, ECE short cert, extra ECE-initial staff, 35 sqft/child indoor (+15 under-24mo), 75 sqft outdoor or rotation, building-code docs, sprinkler generally required. Ratios at waiver: 1:8 ages 2+; 1:6 under-2; max 6 under-2. Waivers >16 must convert/shrink by 2026-06-30.
- Residency mandatory (RCW 43.216.010; WAC 110-300-0005 family living quarters where licensee resides). Staff children on premises count.
- Center (WAC 110-300-0356): infant 1:4 group 8 (or 1:3 group 9); toddler 1:7 group 14 (or 1:5 group 15); preschool 1:10 group 20; school-age 1:15 group 30; mixed-age only first/last 2 hrs at youngest ratio.
- Center in a private residence EXPRESSLY allowed (WAC 110-300-0010(4)): child space exclusive-use during operation or separate from living quarters. No residency requirement. Director: ECE state cert (by Aug 2026) + 2 yrs teaching + 6 mo admin; lead teachers ECE initial.
- License attaches to facility, not person (110-300-0011): relocation = new site application + inspection; person credentials portable (background, MERIT, certs). Decision within 90 days of complete application (RCW 43.216.305); initial license up to 6 mo, renewable to 2 yrs total (43.216.315).

## v4 scenario grid at host card ($2,000 npt / $1,500 pt; npt treated as under-2/infant band)

- CA-L14: 14 kids, 3 npt + 11 pt = $22,500/mo gross, 2 staff (licensee + assistant), by-right, day-one licensed provider assumed to hold large-qualifying experience.
- CA-L12: 12 kids, 4 npt + 8 pt = $20,000/mo gross, 2 staff. (L14 dominates on revenue; L12 avoids seat-13/14 composition conditions.)
- WA-H12: 12 kids, 6 npt + 6 pt = $21,000/mo gross, 2 staff (2+ yr licensee + assistant).
- WA-H16 (waiver): 16 kids, 6 npt + 10 pt = $27,000/mo gross, staffing per 1:6 under-2 + 1:8 over-2 (3 staff), sprinkler capex adder (~$5-15K), 3+ yr licensee.
- WA-C28 (center-in-residence, optimized scenario): e.g., 8 infants (2 staff) + 20 preschool (2 staff) = 28 kids, 4 staff + director = $46,000/mo gross; exclusive-use space; director cert; no residency requirement (removes owner-occupied constraint); fire/building compliance capex adder.
- Flags: npt==under-2 mapping is an assumption (potty training typically 2-3; some npt kids are 2+, which relaxes infant caps and raises achievable npt count); WA experience tiers bypassed by day-one-licensed assumption only if recruited providers actually have 2-3+ yrs (recruiting filter, not regulation).

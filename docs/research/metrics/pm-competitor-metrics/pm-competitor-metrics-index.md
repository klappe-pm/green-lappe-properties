# Pm-competitor-metrics-index

Catalog of competitor research metrics for King and Snohomish County property management firms. Each metric has a dedicated file with definition, examples, counter-metrics, paired metrics, interpretation guidance, and benchmarks.

## Priority Tiers

- P0: critical. Separates real operators from cosmetic ones. Always populate.
- P1: important. Populate during competitive deep-dive.
- P2: supporting. Populate for full diligence on top candidates.

## Metrics by Category

### Compliance and Risk

- [[ag-complaints|AG Complaints]] (P1): Washington State Attorney General consumer protection complaints filed against the firm.
- [[bbb-complaint-resolution-rate|BBB Complaint Resolution Rate]] (P2): Complaint count and resolution status (resolved vs unresolved) on the BBB profile.
- [[doj-hud-complaints|DOJ HUD Complaints]] (P0): Department of Justice or HUD fair housing or civil rights complaints.
- [[designated-broker-license-and-tenure|Designated Broker License and Tenure]] (P0): The named designated broker on the WA DOL firm license, and that broker's tenure.
- [[e-o-insurance-carrier-and-limits|E&O Insurance Carrier and Limits]] (P1): Errors and omissions insurance carrier and coverage limits.
- [[eviction-lawsuits-filed|Eviction Lawsuits Filed]] (P1): Unlawful detainer cases filed by the firm in the trailing 12 months.
- [[fair-housing-training-cadence|Fair Housing Training Cadence]] (P1): Frequency of Fair Housing training for staff, especially leasing agents and property managers.
- [[gl-cyber-coverage|GL Cyber Coverage]] (P2): General liability and cyber liability insurance carrier and limits.
- [[hb-1217-compliance-posture|HB 1217 Compliance Posture]] (P0): Adherence to Washington's 2025 statewide rent stabilization (HB 1217) limiting annual rent increases.
- [[litigation-history|Litigation History]] (P1): Civil cases naming the firm as defendant, especially habitability, security deposit, and discrimination matters.
- [[seattle-compliance-posture|Seattle Compliance Posture]] (P0): Adherence to Seattle-specific ordinances: SMC 14.09 Fair Chance Housing, RRIO, First-in-Time, Just Cause, 180-day rent increase notice.
- [[security-deposit-return-disputes|Security Deposit Return Disputes]] (P2): Number of formal disputes over security deposit returns annually.
- [[trust-account-audit-history|Trust Account Audit History]] (P0): WA DOL trust account audit history and findings.
- [[wa-dol-firm-license-status|WA DOL Firm License Status]] (P0): Active Washington Department of Licensing firm real estate license.

### Financial Performance

- [[ancillary-revenue-per-unit|Ancillary Revenue per Unit]] (P0): Annual non-management-fee revenue per managed unit, including pet fees, late fees, lease prep charges, NSF fees, eviction processing, and similar line items.
- [[average-management-fee-realized|Average Management Fee Realized]] (P0): Effective management fee actually collected as a percentage of rent, after discounts, minimums, vacancies, and ancillaries are netted out. The realized fee usually differs from the advertised fee.
- [[customer-acquisition-cost-per-owner|Customer Acquisition Cost per Owner]] (P1): Total sales and marketing spend divided by the number of new owner accounts won in a period.
- [[ebitda-margin|EBITDA Margin]] (P1): Operating earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation, and amortization, as a percent of revenue.
- [[gross-revenue-per-unit|Gross Revenue per Unit]] (P0): Total annual revenue divided by units under management. Includes management fees, leasing fees, ancillary income.
- [[ltv-per-owner|LTV per Owner]] (P1): Total revenue expected from one owner relationship over its lifetime. Average management revenue per owner per year times average owner tenure.
- [[ltv-to-cac-ratio|LTV to CAC Ratio]] (P1): Lifetime value per owner divided by cost to acquire that owner.
- [[net-unit-growth-rate|Net Unit Growth Rate]] (P0): Units added minus units lost over trailing 12 months, divided by starting unit count.
- [[owner-churn-rate|Owner Churn Rate]] (P0): Percentage of owner accounts that terminate the management agreement annually. Often tracked as logo churn (count of owners) and unit churn (count of doors).
- [[payback-period-on-new-owner|Payback Period on New Owner]] (P1): Number of months for the gross profit from one new owner to cover the acquisition cost.
- [[revenue-mix|Revenue Mix]] (P1): Percentage breakdown of revenue across management fees, leasing fees, ancillary, and maintenance markup.

### Growth and Corporate Development

- [[acquisition-activity|Acquisition Activity]] (P2): Roll-up activity in the trailing 5 years (firms acquired or merged).
- [[affiliate-network|Affiliate Network]] (P2): Network of insurance, lending, title, and vendor partners.
- [[brokerage-tie-in|Brokerage Tie-In]] (P2): Whether the firm operates a co-located sales brokerage.
- [[founding-partner-exit-risk|Founding Partner Exit Risk]] (P1): Risk that the founder or managing partner will exit the firm in the near term.
- [[investor-relationships|Investor Relationships]] (P2): Whether the firm manages a portfolio for related investors, including the firm principals.
- [[new-construction-lease-up-capability|New Construction Lease-Up Capability]] (P2): Demonstrated capability to do lease-up of newly built multifamily or build-to-rent product.
- [[succession-plan|Succession Plan]] (P1): Documented succession arrangement for the principal and designated broker roles.

### Operational Performance

- [[application-to-lease-conversion-rate|Application-to-Lease Conversion Rate]] (P1): Percentage of submitted rental applications that result in a signed lease.
- [[average-tenancy-length|Average Tenancy Length]] (P1): Mean tenant tenure across the portfolio, measured in months.
- [[days-on-market|Days on Market]] (P0): Average days from a unit being listed for rent until a lease is signed.
- [[delinquency-rate|Delinquency Rate]] (P0): Percent of rent more than 30 days past due as of month end.
- [[emergency-response-time|Emergency Response Time]] (P1): Time from emergency maintenance request submission to first dispatched response.
- [[eviction-rate-per-100-units|Eviction Rate per 100 Units]] (P1): Number of unlawful detainer cases filed annually per 100 units under management.
- [[first-time-fix-rate|First-Time Fix Rate]] (P2): Percent of work orders resolved on the first vendor visit without revisit.
- [[inspection-cadence|Inspection Cadence]] (P2): Frequency of formal property inspections (move-in, move-out, periodic, drive-by).
- [[lease-compliance-incidents|Lease Compliance Incidents]] (P1): Number of legal exposure incidents per year (violations of local ordinance, fair housing complaints, security deposit disputes, late notices not properly served).
- [[maintenance-cost-per-unit|Maintenance Cost per Unit]] (P1): Total annual maintenance spend per managed unit, owner-billed.
- [[maintenance-work-order-cycle-time|Maintenance Work Order Cycle Time]] (P1): Average days from work order opened to closed (excluding emergencies).
- [[renewal-rate|Renewal Rate]] (P1): Percent of leases that renew at end of term (rather than the tenant moving out).
- [[rent-collection-rate|Rent Collection Rate]] (P0): Percent of rent collected on time (typically by the 5th of the month), with secondary cuts at day 10 and month-end.
- [[showings-per-vacancy|Showings per Vacancy]] (P2): Average number of showings conducted before a unit leases.
- [[time-to-first-showing|Time to First Showing]] (P2): Hours or days from listing publication to first scheduled showing.
- [[vacancy-rate|Vacancy Rate]] (P0): Percent of portfolio days vacant on a trailing 12-month basis.

### Portfolio Composition

- [[average-doors-per-owner|Average Doors per Owner]] (P1): Mean number of managed units per owner client.
- [[average-rent-per-unit|Average Rent per Unit]] (P2): Mean monthly rent across the managed portfolio.
- [[class-a-b-c-mix|Class A B C Mix]] (P2): Percent of portfolio by property class tier.
- [[geographic-concentration|Geographic Concentration]] (P1): Units per ZIP code or top-ZIP share of total portfolio.
- [[owner-concentration|Owner Concentration]] (P1): Percent of total revenue or units owned by the top 10 owners.
- [[owner-type-mix|Owner Type Mix]] (P2): Share of owners by category: mom-and-pop (1-3 doors), mid-tier (4-20), institutional (20+).
- [[property-type-mix|Property Type Mix]] (P1): Percent split across SFR, condo, townhome, small multifamily, large multifamily, commercial, HOA.

### Reputation and Customer Experience

- [[bbb-complaint-count|BBB Complaint Count]] (P2): Number of BBB complaints filed, not just the letter grade.
- [[facebook-rating-and-count|Facebook Rating and Count]] (P2): Facebook recommendation score and number of recommendations.
- [[google-review-count|Google Review Count]] (P0): Total Google Business Profile reviews.
- [[owner-nps|Owner NPS]] (P0): Net Promoter Score from owner survey.
- [[owner-referral-rate|Owner Referral Rate]] (P1): Percent of new owners that come via referral from existing owners.
- [[repeat-owner-rate|Repeat-Owner Rate]] (P1): Percent of owners with 2 or more managed properties at the firm.
- [[review-velocity|Review Velocity]] (P2): New reviews in trailing 12 months vs prior 12 months across platforms.
- [[tenant-nps|Tenant NPS]] (P1): Net Promoter Score from tenant survey.
- [[yelp-review-count|Yelp Review Count]] (P0): Total Yelp reviews.

### Service and Pricing Depth

- [[contract-terms|Contract Terms]] (P0): Cancellation notice required, exclusivity clauses, lockup terms, holdover claims, termination fees.
- [[inspection-types-included|Inspection Types Included]] (P2): Which inspection types are bundled into management fee vs upcharged (move-in, move-out, periodic, drive-by, emergency).
- [[maintenance-markup-transparency|Maintenance Markup Transparency]] (P0): Whether the firm marks up vendor invoices before passing to owner, and whether the markup is disclosed.
- [[owner-portal-features-depth|Owner Portal Features Depth]] (P2): Functional depth of the owner-facing portal: statements, real-time accounting, document storage, work order visibility, mobile app, owner pay-in/distribution flows.
- [[owner-reporting-cadence|Owner Reporting Cadence]] (P2): Frequency and granularity of owner financial reporting.
- [[performance-guarantees|Performance Guarantees]] (P1): Service guarantees offered: tenant lease guarantee, pet damage guarantee, eviction protection, happiness guarantee, leasing speed guarantee.
- [[pricing-transparency|Pricing Transparency]] (P0): Whether all fees are published on the website vs quote-only or buried in the agreement.
- [[tax-reporting-included|Tax Reporting Included]] (P2): Whether 1099-MISC issuance and end-of-year tax reporting are included in management fee.
- [[trust-accounting-practices|Trust Accounting Practices]] (P0): Whether owner funds are held in separate trust accounts per owner vs commingled, in compliance with WA RCW 18.85.

### Strategic Positioning

- [[content-velocity|Content Velocity]] (P2): Volume of blog, video, podcast, or social content published per quarter.
- [[differentiation-claim|Differentiation Claim]] (P1): The stated competitive positioning vs the rest of the market.
- [[domain-authority|Domain Authority]] (P2): Moz or Ahrefs domain authority score (0-100).
- [[marketing-channels-active|Marketing Channels Active]] (P1): Active acquisition channels: SEO, SEM, referral programs, NARPM, partnerships, content.
- [[seo-rank-for-city-plus-pm|SEO Rank for City Plus PM]] (P2): Search ranking for '[city] property management' queries.
- [[social-media-presence|Social Media Presence]] (P2): Active social profiles on Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube, TikTok.
- [[stated-icp|Stated ICP]] (P1): The firm's stated ideal customer profile.

### Team and Capacity

- [[bilingual-staff-languages|Bilingual Staff Languages]] (P2): Languages other than English spoken by staff with native or working fluency.
- [[bookkeepers-trust-accountants|Bookkeepers Trust Accountants]] (P2): Number of accounting and trust function staff.
- [[designations-held|Designations Held]] (P1): Industry designations held by firm and staff: CRMC, AMO, ARM, MPM, RMP, CCIM.
- [[doors-per-property-manager|Doors per Property Manager]] (P0): Total managed units divided by the number of property managers.
- [[leasing-agent-count|Leasing Agent Count]] (P2): Number of dedicated leasing staff.
- [[maintenance-staff-in-house-vs-outsourced|Maintenance Staff In-House vs Outsourced]] (P1): Whether maintenance technicians are W-2 employees or all work is vendor-routed.
- [[narpm-irem-ccim-designations|NARPM IREM CCIM Designations]] (P1): Specific staff-held designations from NARPM, IREM, and CCIM.
- [[property-manager-count|Property Manager Count]] (P0): Total number of licensed property managers at the firm.
- [[tenure-of-leadership|Tenure of Leadership]] (P1): Years the current principal or executive team has led the firm.
- [[total-headcount|Total Headcount]] (P1): Full-time equivalents at the firm.

### Technology and Process Maturity

- [[ai-leasing-chatbot|AI Leasing Chatbot]] (P2): Use of automated chat/voice agents for inquiry response, scheduling, and qualification.
- [[communication-sla|Communication SLA]] (P1): Committed response time for owner and tenant communications.
- [[core-pms|Core PMS]] (P0): Core property management software platform (AppFolio, Buildium, Rentvine, Rentec, Propertyware, Rent Manager, etc.).
- [[integrations|Integrations]] (P2): Active integrations with accounting (QuickBooks), screening (TransUnion, Experian), insurance, utilities, smart locks.
- [[maintenance-triage-automation|Maintenance Triage Automation]] (P1): Platform automating maintenance request intake, triage, vendor dispatch (Property Meld, Latchel, Lula, EZ Repair Hotline).
- [[online-application-and-e-signature|Online Application and E-signature]] (P1): End-to-end digital application, screening, and lease execution with e-signature.
- [[owner-portal|Owner Portal]] (P1): Owner-facing portal with real-time accounting, statements, work orders, and mobile access.
- [[self-showing-technology|Self-Showing Technology]] (P1): Unattended-showing platform (Showdigs, Tenant Turner, Rently, ShowMojo).
- [[tenant-mobile-app|Tenant Mobile App]] (P2): Branded or platform-native mobile app for tenant payments, work orders, communication.
- [[vendor-management-system|Vendor Management System]] (P2): System for vendor onboarding, COI tracking, performance scoring, payment routing.

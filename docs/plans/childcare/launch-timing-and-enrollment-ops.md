# Launch Timing And Enrollment Ops

- Date: 2026-07-20
- Status: DRAFT
- Parent: [[../childcare-operator-property-model-strategy|Childcare Operator Property Model Strategy]]
- Implements: Launch and enrollment timing

## Objective

Stage operator move-in and capacity so a new site reaches its enrollment floor quickly, by timing to the enrollment calendar the market study identifies.

## Scope

In scope: launch timing rules and the first-fill playbook. Out of scope: the enrollment commitment terms (see operator lease and housing terms).

## Steps

1. Time two-to-three-year-old capacity to open ahead of the late-August and September academic-year enrollment peak.
2. Stage infant capacity for the November to March window, when the late-summer birth cohort becomes care-eligible.
3. Build a first-fill playbook for a new operator: local outreach, waitlist capture, and referral into the highest-need infant band.
4. Track fill rate by calendar month so seasonality is expected, not mistaken for underperformance.

## Acceptance

- Each launch has a target enrollment-floor date tied to the calendar, and a first-fill playbook.

## Measures

- Weeks from operator move-in to the enrollment floor.
- Fill rate by calendar month against the expected seasonal curve.

## Open questions

- The seasonal curve is Orange County; confirm it holds under D1 if the target is Washington.
